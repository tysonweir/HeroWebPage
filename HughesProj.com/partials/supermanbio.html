Hero

Superman

Rocketed to Earth as an infant from the doomed planet Krypton, Kal-El was adopted by the loving Kent family and raised in America's heartland as Clark Kent. Using his immense solar-fueled powers,he became Superman to defend mankind against all manner of threats while championing truth, justice and the American way!

Top Villian

Darkseid

Worshipped as the 'God of Evil', Darkseid is the supreme monarch of planet Apokolips. Considered one of Superman's greatest adversaries, the greatest enemy of New Genesis and the greatest threat to the DC Universe, he seeks to control all living things with the Anti-Life Equation.

Side Kick

Supergirl

Kara Zor-El is Superman's cousin and last survivor of Krypton's Argo City. She has a brash and defiant personality that she developed in response to the destruction of Krypton. Currently, she is protecting Earth in Superman's stead at his request.
